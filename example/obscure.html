--- Quest 1 - B: Sum stuff and more ---

You want to sum things, but you don't know how to do it. You have a list of numbers, and you want to sum them all. Can you do it?

A bit of context to put some context and story to the quest.

Example:

45
15
20

We sum (45 * 2) + (15 * 2) + (20 * 2)

Will give the result: 160
